About Me

I am a freshman at Princeton University majoring in Economics with a certificate in Finance. Originally from Hunterdon County, NJ, I graduated North Hunterdon High School as salutatorian and have attended New Jersey Governor's School in the Sciences. I am currently the Undergraduate Student Government Class of 2018 Officer and am involved in Tiger Investments, Princeton Model UN, and Princeton Corporate Finance Club. In addition to being well-versed in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Lightroom, I am also experienced with Java to a lesser degree. I am fluent in Mandarin and enjoy reading Chinese novels in my spare time.



Ever since I colored the walls in my house at age four, I have been considered the most "artistic" member of my family. I like art because it is a cathartic process that helps me find my heart and soul. My works of art are fragments of my emotions and aspirations, personality and background - snapshots of who I am. When pieced together, they create a complete picture - an n-dimensional me.



In my artwork, I primarily use acrylic paint, oil paint, ink, and graphite pencil. I also love photography and have photographed my own artwork and the pictures on this website. Recently, I started experimenting with graphic design in Illustrator, designing various logos and vector art. I have much to improve on and hope to become more practiced in illustration and various forms of art.
